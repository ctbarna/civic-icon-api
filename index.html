Civic Icon Replacer

Do You Like Icons? I thought you might…

The folks at the Noun Project and Code for America (with the help of countless volunteer designers) have released an amazing set of civic centered icons. In the spirit of involvement, I have put together a quick jQuery plugin to allow anybody to include these icons anywhere on their websites.
